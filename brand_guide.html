Brand Guide

Thinkbean Values

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create custom websites and web applications which creatively and comprehensively achieve our clients’ business goals.

Core Values

Craft clever, efficient solutions to difficult problems which accomplish the client’s explicit business goals.

Don't wait. Iterate. Launch the MVP ASAP.

(A digital property is never “finished” or “perfect”. Make ‘improvements’ while getting the maximum value out of the current asset.)

Value objectivity. De-value subjectivity. (Make objectively-informed decisions, only.)

Always view the work through user eyes.

Listen, politely, to what the client wants. Deliver, adamantly, what the client needs.

(Okay. Admittedly, this is tongue-in-cheek. In all honesty, though, clients hire us (presumably) because we know how to do our job. If our informed opinions are repeatedly disregarded then we may not be partnered with the right client.)

Logo

Main Logo

This is our full logo. It represents the Thinkbean brand. It originated from the idea of mindfulness and thought we put into our work, since we are based right outside of Boston we found a playful way to represent that in our name.

Logo Versions

Our logo can be used in a few different ways. Always remember to give our logo some room to breathe with a good amount of space all around it.
